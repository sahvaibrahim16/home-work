How do I update my iPhone to the latest iOS version?
Go to Settings > General > Software Update and follow the on-screen instructions to install the latest iOS update.
What should I do if my MacBook is not turning on?
First, check the power connection and hold the power button for 10 seconds. If it still doesn’t turn on, try resetting the SMC or contact Apple Support.
How do I back up my iPhone or iPad?
You can back up using iCloud under Settings > [your name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup, or use Finder (macOS) or iTunes (Windows).
